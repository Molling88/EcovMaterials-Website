The Problem We Address

Electronic waste is one of the fastest‑growing waste streams globally—yet it also contains valuable critical minerals essential to clean energy, advanced manufacturing, and digital infrastructure.

At the same time, supply chains for these materials are increasingly vulnerable, opaque, and environmentally costly.

What EcoV Does

EcoV operates at the coordination layer of the circular economy, collecting, securing, and preparing end‑of‑life electronics into certified, traceable critical minerals feedstock.

Why It Matters

By formalizing recovery pathways, EcoV helps reduce environmental harm, strengthen material security, and enable responsible sourcing.
